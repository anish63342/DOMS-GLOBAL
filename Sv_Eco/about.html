Our Story

Welcome to SV Eco Industires! We are a manufacturing company located in Maheshwaram, Hyderabad, specializing in Wood Plastic Composite (WPC) products. Our journey began in 2022 with a clear goal: to create high-quality, eco-friendly products that are durable and sustainable.

Our WPC products are made from a unique blend of wood fibers and recycled plastic, making them an excellent alternative to traditional wood. By choosing WPC, you not only get a product that lasts longer and requires less maintenance, but you also help reduce waste and preserve our forests.

At SV Eco Industires, we believe in doing our part for the environment. That's why our main motto is to use eco-friendly materials in everything we produce. We are committed to reducing our carbon footprint and promoting sustainability. From the raw materials we source to the processes we use, every step is designed to be as green as possible.

We offer a wide range of WPC products, including decking, cladding, fencing, and more. Whether you're a homeowner looking to improve your outdoor space or a business seeking sustainable building materials, we have something for you.

Thank you for visiting our website. We look forward to helping you with your next project. If you have any questions or need more information, please feel free to contact us. We're here to help!

Our Milestone

At SV Eco Industries, our journey in the world of Wood Plastic Composite (WPC) has been filled with significant milestones that demonstrate our dedication to sustainability, innovation, and customer satisfaction. These achievements showcase our growth and evolution as a leading provider of eco-friendly WPC products. Here is a snapshot of our journey:

Dec-2022

Company Inception

SV Eco Industries was founded with a mission to provide eco-friendly Wood Plastic Composite (WPC) products. Our journey began with a vision to create durable and sustainable solutions for our valued customers.

Mar-2023

Formula Creation

In March 2023, our founder at SV Eco Industries developed a unique formula for creating eco-friendly Wood Plastic Composite (WPC) products. This breakthrough innovation set the stage for our commitment to sustainable manufacturing.

July-2023

Customized Product Delivery

On 12th July 2023, SV Eco Industries achieved a milestone by delivering a customized Wood Plastic Composite (WPC) product to a hospital. This accomplishment marked our capability to cater to unique client needs with eco-friendly solutions.

Oct-2023

Technology Update

In October 2023, SV Eco Industries took a major step forward by adopting high-end technology and installing new machinery. This advancement enhanced our production capabilities, allowing us to offer even more innovative and sustainable WPC products.

Dec-2023

Production of Doors

In December 2023, SV Eco Industries began producing Wood Plastic Composite (WPC) doors. This expansion into a new product line reflected our ongoing commitment to creating versatile and sustainable solutions for a variety of needs.

Jan-2024

Customized Home Decor

Starting in January 2024, SV Eco Industries began designing customized Wood Plastic Composite (WPC) home decor items for clients. This milestone highlighted our growing versatility and commitment to offering tailored, eco-friendly solutions for personalized spaces.
